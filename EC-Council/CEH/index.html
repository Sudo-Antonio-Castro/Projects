Assessment path

Module quizzes

Use each 6-question quiz after you finish a module. They are untimed and meant for quick retention checks.

Midterm review

The midterm pulls 45 questions from the first half of the study bank and works well as a checkpoint after Module VIII.

Final readiness

The timed final covers the full question set and is designed as a full-length practice review across all XV modules.
